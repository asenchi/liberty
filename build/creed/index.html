Apostle's Creed

I believe in God, the Father Almighty, Maker of Heaven and earth.
I also believe in Jesus Christ, His only Son our Lord:
Conceived of the Holy Spirit, born of the Virgin Mary
suffered under Pontius Pilate, crucified, dead and buried.

He descended into Hell; Rose again the third day;
Ascended into Heaven; Sat down at the right hand of the Father.
Thence He will come to judge the living and the dead.

I believe in the Holy Spirit, the holy catholic church
the communion of saints, the remission of sins
the resurrection of flesh and life eternal.

Amen.
